Who We Are

AD Investments — Your Financial Partner

AD Investments is a SEBI/AMFI registered financial distribution firm based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. We work with salaried individuals, business owners, and families to help them navigate the complex world of financial products — from mutual funds and insurance to loans and bonds.

We believe that every person, regardless of income level, deserves access to quality financial advice. Our approach is simple — understand your goals, build a plan, and walk with you every step of the way.

With over 17 financial services under one roof and a growing client base of 50+ satisfied investors, AD Investments is becoming Lucknow's most trusted financial partner.
